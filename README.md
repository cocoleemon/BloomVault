# 🌸 BloomVault

### Your Personal Beauty Library

*Research. Save. Compare. Glow.*

---

BloomVault is a **research-first mobile application** designed for beauty enthusiasts who love discovering, comparing, organizing, and understanding beauty products before purchasing them.

Unlike traditional beauty shopping apps, BloomVault serves as a **personal beauty library** where every saved product has context, every ingredient is easy to understand, and every purchase begins with informed research.

> **Status:** 🚧 Currently in Product Planning (Phase 1)

</div>

---

# ✨ Why BloomVault?

Every beauty enthusiast has experienced this.

You discover an interesting product on TikTok.

Then you...

- 📱 Save the video
- 📸 Screenshot the product
- 📝 Write it in Notes
- 🔍 Search Reddit reviews
- 🧪 Look up ingredients
- 🛒 Add it to your shopping cart
- 🤔 Forget why you saved it

Eventually your research becomes scattered across multiple apps and websites.

BloomVault exists to solve that problem.

Instead of scattered information, BloomVault gives users one beautifully organized place to research, collect, and remember everything related to beauty.

---

# 🌸 Vision

To become the world's most trusted **personal beauty library**.

---

# 🎯 Mission

Empower beauty enthusiasts to make smarter, more confident purchasing decisions through education, organization, and personalized research.

---

# 💡 Product Philosophy

BloomVault is built around five principles.

### 📚 Your library comes first

The first screen should always feel like opening **your own beauty collection**, not an online store.

---

### 🔍 Research before shopping

BloomVault helps users understand products before purchasing them.

---

### 📝 Every saved product tells a story

Users should always remember **why** they saved a product.

---

### 🌿 Calm over clutter

The experience should feel elegant, thoughtful, and organized.

---

### 📖 Education over marketing

Transparent, evidence-based information should always come before trends or promotions.
