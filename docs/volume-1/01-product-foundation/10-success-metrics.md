# 🌸 Success Metrics

> *"Success isn't measured by how many people download the app. It's measured by how naturally it becomes part of their lives."*

---

# Introduction

BloomVault is designed to become an essential companion in every beauty enthusiast's research journey.

Success is not defined solely by downloads or revenue, but by how consistently users return to BloomVault whenever they discover a new beauty product.

The metrics in this document help measure whether BloomVault is fulfilling its purpose and delivering meaningful value to its users.

---

# Our North Star Metric

## 📚 Products Saved to Personal Libraries

The primary indicator of BloomVault's success is the number of products users intentionally save to their personal beauty libraries.

Every saved product represents:

* A moment of curiosity.
* A research journey.
* A future purchasing decision.
* A growing personal knowledge library.

The more users naturally save products to BloomVault, the more deeply the app becomes integrated into their beauty habits.

---

# Product Success Metrics

## Library Growth

Measures how users build their personal beauty libraries over time.

Example metrics include:

* Products saved per user
* Collections created
* Notes added to products
* Products organized into routines
* Wishlist growth

Healthy libraries indicate that users find lasting value in BloomVault.

---

## User Engagement

Measures how frequently users return to BloomVault.

Key indicators include:

* Daily Active Users (DAU)
* Weekly Active Users (WAU)
* Monthly Active Users (MAU)
* Session frequency
* Average session duration

The goal is consistent engagement rather than occasional visits.

---

## Research Activity

Measures whether users actively use BloomVault as a research tool.

Examples include:

* Product searches
* Ingredient views
* Product comparisons
* Collection browsing
* Routine updates

These actions indicate that users are learning—not simply storing products.

---

## User Retention

A valuable library encourages users to return.

Success is measured through:

* Day 1 retention
* Day 7 retention
* Day 30 retention
* Returning users
* Long-term active users

High retention suggests BloomVault has become part of the user's regular beauty routine.

---

## Organization Quality

BloomVault is more than a storage app.

Users should actively organize their libraries.

Indicators include:

* Collections created
* Tags applied
* Personal notes written
* Products categorized
* Favorites marked

Organization demonstrates that users see BloomVault as a personal workspace rather than a temporary wishlist.

---

# User Experience Metrics

A great product is not only useful—it is enjoyable to use.

BloomVault aims to deliver an experience that feels calm, intuitive, and effortless.

Success can be measured through:

* User satisfaction (CSAT)
* Net Promoter Score (NPS)
* App Store ratings
* User feedback
* Feature adoption

Qualitative feedback is just as valuable as quantitative metrics.

---

# Business Metrics

As BloomVault grows, business health becomes increasingly important.

Examples include:

* App downloads
* Account registrations
* Monthly active users
* Premium subscriptions
* Subscription conversion rate
* Revenue growth

These metrics support sustainable development while remaining secondary to product value.

---

# Long-Term Success

BloomVault succeeds when it becomes a natural part of the beauty research journey.

Users instinctively think:

> "I'll save this to BloomVault."

rather than:

> "I'll take a screenshot."

or

> "I'll put it in my Notes."

When BloomVault replaces scattered research habits with an organized personal beauty library, it has achieved its mission.

---

# Measuring What Matters

BloomVault does not aim to maximize screen time.

Instead, it aims to maximize confidence.

The product should help users:

* Make informed beauty decisions.
* Stay organized.
* Learn continuously.
* Build lasting beauty knowledge.

If users leave the app feeling more confident than when they opened it, BloomVault has created meaningful value.

---

# Success Statement

BloomVault's greatest success is not becoming the largest beauty app.

It is becoming the most trusted personal beauty library for every user who loves researching beauty products.

That trust is earned one saved product, one thoughtful note, and one confident decision at a time.
