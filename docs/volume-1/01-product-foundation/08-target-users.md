# 🌸 Target Users

> *"When you build for everyone, you build for no one."*

---

# Introduction

BloomVault is designed for people who view beauty as more than a purchase.

Our users are naturally curious. They enjoy learning about ingredients, comparing products, organizing discoveries, and making thoughtful decisions before adding something to their routine.

Rather than focusing solely on age or gender, BloomVault is built around shared behaviors, motivations, and habits.

---

# Our Primary Audience

BloomVault is designed primarily for **beauty researchers**.

These are people who enjoy discovering, understanding, and organizing beauty products before making a purchase.

Research is not an obstacle.

It is part of the experience.

Whether they spend five minutes or five days comparing products, they value confidence over impulse.

---

# Primary User Groups

## 🌿 Skincare Enthusiasts

Users who actively research skincare ingredients, routines, and product compatibility.

Typical behaviors include:

* Reading ingredient lists
* Comparing active ingredients
* Following dermatologists
* Building skincare routines
* Tracking product performance
* Saving products for future research

Needs:

* Ingredient education
* Routine organization
* Product comparison
* Personal notes

---

## 💄 Beauty Collectors

Users who enjoy saving products, creating wishlists, and organizing beauty discoveries.

They often discover products faster than they purchase them.

Their collections represent inspiration as much as intention.

Needs:

* Beautiful collections
* Wishlist organization
* Product tracking
* Personal library

---

## 🌸 Curious Beginners

Users who are new to skincare or beauty and want trusted guidance.

They often feel overwhelmed by conflicting advice, unfamiliar ingredients, and endless product recommendations.

Needs:

* Beginner-friendly explanations
* Educational content
* Clear product information
* Confidence when making decisions

---

## 🧴 Routine Builders

Users who enjoy creating personalized routines and refining them over time.

They appreciate organization and consistency.

Needs:

* Routine management
* Product organization
* Usage tracking
* Personal notes

---

## 🌺 Fragrance & Haircare Lovers

Although BloomVault begins with beauty products broadly, many users build collections around specific interests.

Examples include:

* Perfume collections
* Haircare routines
* Body care favorites
* Makeup wishlists

BloomVault is designed to support every category within a unified personal library.

# User Goals

BloomVault helps users achieve goals such as:

* Organize beauty research
* Understand ingredients
* Compare products confidently
* Build personalized routines
* Keep track of favorite products
* Remember why products were saved
* Reduce decision fatigue
* Purchase more intentionally

---

# User Pain Points

Without BloomVault, users often experience:

* Information scattered across multiple apps
* Forgotten notes and screenshots
* Confusing ingredient lists
* Difficulty comparing similar products
* Overwhelming wishlists
* Repeating the same research
* Uncertainty before purchasing

These frustrations guide BloomVault's product decisions.

---

# Who BloomVault Is Not For

BloomVault is intentionally not designed for users who:

* Want the fastest possible shopping experience
* Prefer impulse purchases over research
* Use beauty apps only for discounts or promotions
* Expect an influencer-driven social platform
* Have little interest in organizing products or learning about ingredients

This focus allows BloomVault to remain true to its research-first philosophy.

---

# Our Ideal User

The ideal BloomVault user is someone who naturally says:

> "Before I buy this, I want to learn more about it."

They enjoy discovering products.

They appreciate understanding ingredients.

They organize their thoughts.

They save products with intention.

They believe that confidence comes from knowledge.

BloomVault exists to support that journey.

---

# Success Definition

We know we are serving the right audience when users instinctively open BloomVault whenever they discover a new beauty product.

The app becomes their first stop for research—not their last stop before checkout.
