# 🌸 User Personas

> *"Products are built for people, not demographics."*

---

# Introduction

BloomVault is designed for users who approach beauty with curiosity, intention, and a desire to learn.

These personas represent the different types of users BloomVault serves. They help guide product decisions, user experience, and feature prioritization by keeping real user needs at the center of development.

Although fictional, each persona reflects behaviors and challenges commonly experienced by beauty enthusiasts.

---

# Persona 1 — Sophia, The Beauty Researcher

**Age:** 27

**Occupation:** Software Engineer

**Beauty Interests:** Skincare, haircare, body care, fragrances

### About

Sophia enjoys researching beauty products as a hobby.

Before purchasing anything, she compares ingredients, watches reviews, reads community discussions, and keeps detailed Notes on products she's interested in.

She has hundreds of screenshots and multiple Notes filled with product lists, making it difficult to keep everything organized.

### Goals

* Organize beauty research in one place.
* Compare products before purchasing.
* Remember why products were saved.
* Build a thoughtful beauty collection.

### Frustrations

* Too many screenshots.
* Scattered Notes.
* Forgetting why products were saved.
* Repeating research.

### How BloomVault Helps

BloomVault becomes Sophia's personal beauty library, replacing screenshots and Notes with organized collections, product comparisons, and searchable research.

---

# Persona 2 — Mia, The Skincare Beginner

**Age:** 22

**Occupation:** University Student

**Beauty Interests:** Acne care, sunscreen, beginner skincare

### About

Mia recently became interested in skincare.

She wants to build a simple routine but feels overwhelmed by unfamiliar ingredient names, conflicting advice, and endless product recommendations online.

### Goals

* Learn skincare fundamentals.
* Understand ingredients.
* Build her first routine.
* Purchase products with confidence.

### Frustrations

* Too much conflicting information.
* Scientific ingredient names.
* Fear of buying the wrong products.
* Information overload.

### How BloomVault Helps

BloomVault provides beginner-friendly explanations, educational product pages, and organized routines that make skincare easier to understand.

---

# Persona 3 — Ava, The Beauty Collector

**Age:** 30

**Occupation:** Marketing Manager

**Beauty Interests:** Makeup, perfumes, luxury skincare

### About

Ava loves discovering beautiful products and curating collections.

She enjoys saving products she hopes to purchase in the future and takes pride in organizing her beauty favorites.

### Goals

* Organize beauty collections.
* Track products she owns.
* Build curated wishlists.
* Keep a record of favorites.

### Frustrations

* Wishlists become cluttered.
* Difficult to organize products by category.
* Forgetting previously saved products.

### How BloomVault Helps

Collections, folders, tags, and personal libraries transform Ava's beauty discoveries into an organized collection she enjoys revisiting.

# Persona 4 — Emma, The Routine Optimizer

**Age:** 34

**Occupation:** Healthcare Professional

**Beauty Interests:** Sensitive skin, anti-aging, evidence-based skincare

### About

Emma enjoys refining her skincare routine over time.

She carefully researches ingredients, introduces products gradually, and keeps notes on what works for her skin.

### Goals

* Build effective routines.
* Track product usage.
* Understand ingredient compatibility.
* Document skin progress.

### Frustrations

* Forgetting previous experiences.
* Difficulty tracking products.
* Remembering product combinations.

### How BloomVault Helps

Routine management, product history, ingredient education, and personal journaling help Emma continuously improve her skincare routine.

# Persona 5 — Chloe, The Beauty Creator

**Age:** 26

**Occupation:** Beauty Content Creator

**Beauty Interests:** Product reviews, trends, ingredient education

### About

Chloe creates beauty content across social media.

She constantly researches products, compares formulations, and organizes ideas for future content.

She needs quick access to reliable product information.

### Goals

* Organize beauty research.
* Quickly compare products.
* Save products for future reviews.
* Reference ingredient information efficiently.

### Frustrations

* Constantly switching between websites.
* Losing research.
* Managing multiple spreadsheets and Notes.

### How BloomVault Helps

BloomVault becomes Chloe's central workspace for organizing research, preparing content, and accessing product information.

# Common Characteristics

Despite their differences, every BloomVault user shares similar behaviors.

They:

* Research before purchasing.
* Value organization.
* Enjoy learning.
* Save products intentionally.
* Appreciate thoughtful design.
* Want confidence in their beauty decisions.

These shared traits shape every feature BloomVault creates.

---

# Design Implications

These personas influence how BloomVault is designed.

As a result, the product should:

* Prioritize the user's personal library.
* Make information easy to understand.
* Reduce decision fatigue.
* Preserve personal notes and context.
* Encourage thoughtful exploration.
* Feel welcoming to both beginners and experienced enthusiasts.

Every design decision should answer one question:

**"Would this make life easier for one of our personas?"**

# Closing

BloomVault is built for people who believe beauty research is more than preparation—it is part of the experience.

By understanding the goals, challenges, and motivations of our users, BloomVault creates a product that feels personal, intentional, and genuinely helpful.
