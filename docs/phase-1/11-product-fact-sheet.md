# Product Summary

BloomVault is a research-first mobile application designed for beauty enthusiasts who love discovering, organizing, and understanding beauty products before making a purchase.

Instead of acting as an online marketplace, BloomVault serves as a personal beauty library where users can save products, compare ingredients, organize collections, build routines, and document their beauty journey in one place.

---

# Vision

To become the world's most trusted personal beauty library where every beauty decision begins with knowledge.

---

# Mission

To empower beauty enthusiasts with a beautiful, intelligent, and personalized platform for researching, organizing, and understanding beauty products.

---

# Product Positioning

BloomVault is:

* A personal beauty library

BloomVault is **not**:

* A discount marketplace
