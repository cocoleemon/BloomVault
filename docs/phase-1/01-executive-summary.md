# BloomVault

**Your Personal Beauty Library**

## Overview

BloomVault is a research-first mobile application designed for beauty enthusiasts who enjoy discovering, comparing, organizing, and understanding beauty products before making a purchase.

Unlike traditional beauty shopping platforms or skincare routine trackers, BloomVault serves as a personal beauty library where users can save products, build collections, compare ingredients, create routines, record personal notes, and organize their beauty journey in one beautifully curated space.

BloomVault was created around a simple idea:

> **Beauty research deserves a home.**

Today, beauty enthusiasts rely on multiple platforms to research products—including social media, ingredient databases, online communities, shopping apps, browser bookmarks, screenshots, and note-taking applications. As a result, valuable research becomes fragmented, difficult to revisit, and easy to forget.

BloomVault centralizes that experience into a single application designed specifically for people who enjoy researching before they buy.

Rather than encouraging impulse purchases, BloomVault empowers users to make thoughtful, informed decisions through education, organization, and personalized recommendations.

# The Opportunity

The beauty industry continues to grow rapidly, with new products launching daily across skincare, makeup, haircare, body care, and fragrance.

At the same time, consumers are becoming more informed. They compare ingredients, seek community opinions, watch educational content, and spend significant time researching before making a purchase.

Despite this shift, there is no single platform that combines:

* Product organization
* Ingredient education
* Product comparison
* Personal collections
* Routine management
* Personal notes
* Beauty journaling

BloomVault fills this gap by becoming the user's trusted beauty companion throughout their entire research journey.
